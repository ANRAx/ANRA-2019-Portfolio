Night Owl, Early Bird Who am I?

I am a Fullstack Developer currently located in New York and constantly looking for efficient solutions to complex problems. I have worked in various professional industries and roles from creative marketing and help desk technician to logistics coordinator. My greatest passion in life has been found both inside and outside of the office: problem-solving.

With an insatiable curiosity towards the unknown, I find the notion of jumping into the water to see what can be learned very captivating. During my time at Columbia University Coding Bootcamp, I found a love for working in groups with my colleagues and discovering what solutions can be created when curious minds are put together. My passion for problem solving and collaboration drives me closer to my goal of being a people oriented developer.

When not writing code, I enjoy spending my time writing screenplays, exploring film making, messing around with the Unity and Unreal Game Engines, playing and recording music, and building PC's.
